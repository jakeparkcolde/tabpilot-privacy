CORE FEATURES

Tab cleanup that stays out of your workflow

01

Safety rules first

Active, pinned, audible, loading tabs, and protected sites are excluded from cleanup.

02

Sleep, don't close

TabPilot uses Chrome's native sleeping behavior to keep tabs and URLs available while releasing memory.

03

Restore anytime

Reopen sleeping tabs or undo the most recent cleanup in one step.

04

Conditional automatic cleanup

Runs only when enabled by the user and available system memory is below the configured threshold.

05

Configure your way

Choose from three popup layouts and system, light, or dark theme.

06

Local history management

Review sleeping tabs and cleanup history in one place, and delete records anytime.

PRIVACY BY DESIGN

Your tab data does not leave your device.

TabPilot does not use accounts, analytics tools, ad SDKs, or remote code. Tab titles and URLs are used only to determine cleanup candidates.
